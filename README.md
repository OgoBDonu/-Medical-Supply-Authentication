# Medical Supply Authentication

A blockchain-based solution for verifying the authenticity of medications and medical supplies, combating counterfeit pharmaceuticals by tracking products from manufacturer to patient.

## Overview

This smart contract system provides a secure and transparent way to track medical supplies throughout the supply chain. By recording each step from manufacturer to patient, it helps ensure the authenticity of medical products and reduces the risk of counterfeit pharmaceuticals entering the supply chain.

## Features

- **Product Registration**: Manufacturers can register new medical products with detailed information
- **Supply Chain Tracking**: Record each step in the supply chain with location and handler information
- **Ownership Transfer**: Transfer ownership of products between authorized entities
- **Authentication Verification**: Verify the authenticity of medical products
- **Role-Based Access Control**: Different permissions for manufacturers, distributors, and healthcare providers

## Technical Details

The system is built using Clarity, a decidable smart contract language for the Stacks blockchain, and developed with Clarinet.
